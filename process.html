Your Surrogacy Journey — Step by Step

Each journey is managed through a structured sequence of legal, medical, and coordination stages.

While every case is individual, the process follows a clearly defined framework designed to ensure continuity, control, and transparency from beginning to completion.

01

Initial Consultation

Your journey begins with a confidential consultation to understand your personal situation, medical background, and family planning goals.

At this stage, you receive a structured overview of available programs, expected timelines, and the most suitable approach for your case.

02

Case Planning & Program Structuring

Following the consultation, your case is reviewed in detail to determine the appropriate program structure.

This includes evaluation of medical factors, donor requirements, legal considerations, and overall process strategy to ensure a coordinated approach from the outset.

03

Donor & Medical Preparation

If donor support is required, suitable donor options are identified and coordinated.

At the same time, medical preparation is organized to ensure readiness for the IVF stage, with all procedures planned within a controlled framework.

04

Legal Structuring

All required legal agreements and documentation are prepared and executed in accordance with applicable regulations.

This stage ensures that the roles, responsibilities, and rights of all parties are clearly defined before proceeding further.

05

Embryo Creation & Testing

Embryos are created under clinical supervision through the IVF process.

Where included in the program, genetic testing (PGT/NGS) may be performed to support informed embryo selection and optimize outcomes.

06

Surrogate Matching & Preparation

A suitable surrogate is selected based on medical criteria and program requirements.

The surrogate is then prepared for the transfer process, including medical evaluation and procedural readiness.

07

Embryo Transfer & Pregnancy Confirmation

The selected embryo is transferred, followed by confirmation of pregnancy.

Once pregnancy is established, the process transitions into a structured monitoring phase.

08

Pregnancy Monitoring

Throughout the pregnancy, medical supervision is maintained with regular updates and coordinated follow-up.

Each stage is managed to ensure stability, transparency, and continuous communication.

09

Birth & Legal Documentation

Delivery is organized in coordination with medical providers and relevant professionals.

Following birth, all required documentation is prepared, including official birth registration and supporting legal documents.

10

Exit Process & Return Home

The final stage includes completion of administrative procedures and coordination with relevant authorities.

This ensures that all documentation is finalized, allowing you to return home with your child in a structured and compliant manner.
