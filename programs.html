Standard Program

Structured Foundation for the Journey

A fully structured program covering all key stages of the surrogacy process within a coordinated framework.

Includes:

  • IVF and embryo transfer procedures
  • Surrogate matching, screening, and coordination
  • Legal structuring and documentation
  • Pregnancy monitoring through delivery
  • Birth organization and documentation support

Best suited for:

  • Intended parents seeking a structured and well-managed process
  • Cases with clear medical direction
  • Clients looking for a balanced and efficient approach
