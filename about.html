VitaBridge Consulting

VitaBridge Consulting is a coordination and consulting practice focused on managing surrogacy journeys in Armenia for international intended parents.

Our role is to structure and oversee the process across legal, medical, and logistical stages — ensuring that each step is aligned, controlled, and professionally managed from beginning to completion.
