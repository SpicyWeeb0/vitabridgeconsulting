Who We Are

A structured approach to guiding intended parents through the surrogacy journey with clarity, control, and professional coordination.

VitaBridge Consulting

VitaBridge Consulting is a coordination and consulting practice focused on managing surrogacy journeys in Armenia for international intended parents.

Our role is to structure and oversee the process across legal, medical, and logistical stages — ensuring that each step is aligned, controlled, and professionally managed from beginning to completion.

A Structured and Controlled Approach

Surrogacy is a multi-stage process involving multiple participants and dependencies. Without proper coordination, the process can become fragmented and unpredictable.

Our approach is based on:

Clear Structuring

Clear program structuring

Continuous Coordination

Continuous coordination across all stages

Full Alignment

Alignment between legal, medical, and logistical components

No Delays

Maintaining continuity without unnecessary delays

Each journey is managed as a unified process rather than a series of disconnected steps.

Experience Behind the Process

Surrogacy is not a sequence of independent steps, but a complex process where each stage directly impacts the next. Managing this process effectively requires more than structure — it requires experience in how these stages interact in real conditions.

Our work is supported by over 15 years of direct operational experience in surrogacy and reproductive program management, including involvement at clinical and coordination levels.
