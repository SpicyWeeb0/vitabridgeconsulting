Is Surrogacy Legal in Armenia?

Surrogacy in Armenia is conducted within a defined legal framework regulating assisted reproductive technologies.

For intended parents, understanding the legal structure is essential to ensure that the process is organized correctly and aligned with applicable procedures.

When properly structured, the legal component of surrogacy provides clarity in roles, responsibilities, and parental rights.

Legal Status of Surrogacy

Surrogacy is regulated under laws governing assisted reproductive technologies and related medical procedures.

The process is structured through:

  • Formal agreements between intended parents and the surrogate
  • Defined legal procedures for establishing parental rights
  • Documentation and registration processes following birth

Each case is organized in accordance with applicable regulations to ensure compliance and legal clarity.

Legal Structure of the Process

The legal framework is designed to support a structured and coordinated process.

This includes:

  • Preparation and execution of legal agreements
  • Alignment between medical procedures and legal documentation
  • Registration of parental rights following delivery

All legal steps are integrated into the overall process to ensure continuity and proper sequencing.

Role of Legal Professionals

The legal component of the process is coordinated with experienced Armenian legal professionals specialized in surrogacy-related matters.

Their involvement includes:

  • Structuring and reviewing agreements
  • Managing documentation and compliance procedures
  • Supporting parental rights establishment
  • Ensuring alignment with applicable legal requirements

This ensures that the legal framework is not only compliant but actively managed throughout the process.

Parental Rights

Parental rights are established through defined legal procedures following birth.

This process typically includes:

  • Execution of pre-arranged legal documentation
  • Registration of the child in accordance with applicable regulations
  • Formal recognition of intended parents

The structure of the process allows parental rights to be clearly defined and documented. For the complete legal overview, see our legal framework page.

Legal Clarity and Process Control

Legal clarity plays a critical role in maintaining control over the surrogacy journey.

A properly structured legal framework allows:

  • Clear definition of roles and responsibilities
  • Reduced uncertainty during the process
  • Alignment between all involved parties
  • Controlled execution of each stage

Important Considerations

While the legal framework provides structure, each case is individual and may require tailored legal coordination.

Factors may include:

  • Medical specifics
  • Individual circumstances of intended parents
  • Process structure and program selection

For this reason, legal planning is integrated into the overall process from the beginning.

Conclusion

Surrogacy in Armenia is supported by a structured legal framework that allows the process to be organized with clarity and coordination.
